Our Journey

1

The Idea of AZCA

The idea of ​​the Alzaiem Alzhari Computer Association began because of a problem faced by 10 students of the 16th batch due to their violation of one of the university’s regulations... where the college administration decided to stop the students from graduating with their colleagues.

Date: 2011
2

Foundation

The students of the 16th batch decided to hold a sit-in in order to pressure the college administration to regularize the status of their fellow students so that they can graduate with the batch.

Date: 5Dec-2011
3

Spark Start

The sit-in was successful and the idea of ​​the sit-in led to the establishment of the first general assembly to form a legitimate association.

Date: 8Dec-2011
